Range provides time and location-specific information about free basic human services - food and safe places - for youth in need. It provides a mobile interface to available services for community-based providers like librarians, camp counselors, street outreach workers, church group leaders to be able to locate the closest service. Seed funding is provide by a grant from Microsoft Citizenship.
